SERVICES

Hotel PMS (Property Management System)

Our Hotel PMS is a comprehensive solution designed to streamline hotel operations. It includes automated accounts for calculating taxes, percentages, and expenses. It also manages staff details, schedules, and updates efficiently. The PMS integrates all hotel management tasks into a single system, improving productivity and reducing manual errors. It ensures smoother day-to-day operations and better guest satisfaction.

Channel Manager

The channel manager connects your hotel to multiple booking platforms in real-time. It ensures all availability, pricing, and reservations are automatically synchronized, reducing the risk of overbooking. This feature maximizes occupancy rates by providing instant updates across all platforms. By integrating with our PMS, it simplifies the booking process and enhances revenue management, making it an essential tool for modern hoteliers.

Smart Lock Technology

The smart lock system empowers guests to unlock their rooms using the TuchTrip app, eliminating the need for physical keys. It includes energy management by cutting electricity automatically when rooms are unoccupied, significantly reducing utility costs. This retrofit-friendly solution works with existing RFID locks or as an additional layer for non-RFID systems. By reducing maintenance and enhancing security, the smart lock technology provides convenience for guests and cost savings for hoteliers.

Staff-Side Application

This application ensures seamless communication between guests and staff through instant notifications. Guests can request services or assistance anytime, improving responsiveness and satisfaction. For hotel operations, the app keeps staff updated on tasks, schedules, and priorities. It reduces service delays and ensures accountability, enabling hotels to maintain high standards of service. The platform bridges the communication gap, enhancing both guest experiences and operational efficiency.

Booking and Rewards System

With the TuchTrip app, guests enjoy a streamlined booking process that is simple and quick. The rewards system incentivizes guests by offering loyalty points for bookings, encouraging repeat usage. These rewards add value to their stay, enhancing guest satisfaction. This system also builds a loyal customer base while providing hoteliers with better revenue opportunities. The intuitive interface ensures guests have a hassle-free booking experience while being rewarded for their patronage.
